Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16-03-2025

Welcome to NimbusKit! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and tools, including the Sitemap Dupli Checker Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data from users of our website or tools. However, our Chrome extension may interact with the following data:

  • Sitemap Data: The extension reads the content of `sitemap.xml` files to identify duplicate links. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Browser Permissions: The extension requires access to the active tab and clipboard to function. No data from these permissions is stored or shared.

2. How We Use Information

We do not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive information. The data processed by our tools is used solely for the purpose of providing the functionality described (e.g., identifying duplicate links in `sitemap.xml` files).

3. Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website and tools do not use any third-party services that collect or process user data.

5. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.
